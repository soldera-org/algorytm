## Domain Knowledge - Soldera Platform

### What is Soldera?

Soldera is the leading platform for managing Guarantees of Origin (GOs) across Europe. We help renewable energy producers maximize profits while minimizing administrative workload through intelligent automation.

#### Core Business

- **Automated GO Management**: From registry to revenue on autopilot
- **Trading Optimization**: Maximize returns through aggregated sales to international buyers
- **Revenue Stabilization**: Stabilize revenue streams through forward-looking trading strategies
- **Consolidated Reporting**: Single integrated system across all entities, SPVs, and countries

#### Key Features

- **No Software Fees**: Commission-based model on successful transactions only
- **International Network**: Wide network of international buyers for better pricing
- **Smart Automation**: Automated registry interactions, market access, compliance, contracts, and ecolabels
- **Production Strategy**: Customizable strategies for forward sales, spot sales, PPA reserves, and self-management

### Critical Terminology in Multiple Languages

#### Guarantees of Origin (GO)

This is the MOST IMPORTANT term in our domain. Always use the correct translation:

- **English**: "Guarantees of Origin" (GO)
- **Estonian**: "Päritolutunnistus" (PT)
- **Latvian**: "Izcelsmes apliecinājums" (IA)
- **Lithuanian**: "Kilmės garantija" (KG)
- **Swedish**: "Ursprungsgaranti" (UG)

#### Registry Terminology

- **Registry**: The official government system that issues and tracks GOs
- **Certificate**: Individual GO certificate representing 1 MWh of renewable energy
- **Portfolio**: Collection of GO certificates owned by a company
- **Transfer**: Moving GO certificates between registries/accounts

#### Trading & Strategy Terms

- **Forward Sales**: Pre-selling future production at agreed prices
- **Spot Sales**: Selling certificates at current market prices
- **PPA Reserve**: Setting aside certificates for Power Purchase Agreements
- **Self-Management**: Keeping certificates for own use or manual management
- **Ecolabel**: Additional environmental certification (EKOenergy, Naturemade Star, etc.)

#### Market Participants

- **Producer**: Renewable energy generator who receives GO certificates
- **Buyer**: Company purchasing GO certificates for sustainability reporting
- **Registry**: Official government system managing GO certificates
- **AIB (Association of Issuing Bodies)**: Organization coordinating GO systems across Europe

### Important Notes for AI Assistants

1. **Always use correct terminology** in the user's language
2. **Guarantees of Origin is NOT the same as carbon credits** - they are different instruments
3. **1 GO certificate = 1 MWh** of renewable energy production
4. **Soldera is B2B platform** - we serve renewable energy producers and corporate buyers
5. **Commission-based model** - no upfront fees, only earn on successful transactions
6. **European focus** - primarily serving EU markets with AIB registry integration

### Common Misconceptions to Avoid

- ❌ "Carbon credits" - Use "Guarantees of Origin" or "GO certificates"
- ❌ "Energy trading" - We trade certificates, not physical energy
- ❌ "Blockchain" - Traditional registry systems, not blockchain-based
- ❌ "Retail consumers" - We serve B2B markets, not individual consumers
- ❌ "All renewable energy" - Only certified renewable energy with GO certificates
